### Recommendations
Reading Books, listening to Podcasts, watching Movies, Documentaries and TV Shows is a big part of my learning process. It is impossible to list everything I've read or watched here but this is a _brief_ list of my top recommendations (some of which are just standards but are important). Enjoy!

---

#### Books and Authors

- Biography
	- [The Cuckoo's Egg](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Cuckoo%27s_Egg) - Clifford Stoll
	- [My Inventions](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/My_Inventions:_The_Autobiography_of_Nikola_Tesla) - Nikola Tesla
	- [iWoz](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IWoz) - Steve Wozniak
	- [Permanent Record](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Permanent_Record_(autobiography)) - Edward Snowden
	- [Elon Musk: Tesla, SpaceX, and the Quest for a Fantastic Future](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elon_Musk:_Tesla,_SpaceX,_and_the_Quest_for_a_Fantastic_Future) - Ashlee Vance

- Sci-Fi
	- [The Foundation Series](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Foundation_series) - Isaac Asimov
	- [The Hitchhiker's Guide To The Galaxy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Hitchhiker%27s_Guide_to_the_Galaxy) - Douglas Adams
	- [Snow Crash](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Snow_Crash) - Neal Stephenson
	- [20000 Leagues Under The Sea](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Twenty_Thousand_Leagues_Under_the_Sea) - Jules Verne

- CS and Software Development
	- [The Jargon File](http://www.catb.org/jargon/)

	- [Code Complete](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Code_Complete) - Steve McConnell
	- [The Mythical Man-Month](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Mythical_Man-Month) - Fred Brooks
	- [The Cathedral And The Bazaar](http://www.catb.org/~esr/writings/cathedral-bazaar/) - Eric S. Raymond

- Non-fiction
  - Science
    - [Superintelligence: Paths, Dangers, Strategies](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Superintelligence:_Paths,_Dangers,_Strategies) - Nick Bostrom
    - [The God Delusion](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_God_Delusion) - Richard Dawkins
    - [Just Six Numbers](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/81884.Just_Six_Numbers) - Martin Rees

    - [Fermat's Enigma](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fermat%27s_Last_Theorem_(book)) - Simon Singh
